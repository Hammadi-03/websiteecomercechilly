Our Story

From a backyard obsession to bringing flavor to tables worldwide

Born from Passion

Yellowbird began in 2011 with a simple mission: create hot sauce that's about flavor first, heat second. What started as a backyard experiment in Austin, Texas has grown into a beloved brand known for its commitment to quality ingredients and balanced flavor profiles.

We believe hot sauce should enhance your food, not overpower it. That's why we source the freshest peppers, fruits, and vegetables, blending them in small batches to ensure every bottle delivers the perfect balance of heat and flavor.

Our name comes from the joyful, adventurous spirit we bring to every creation. Like a yellowbird in flight, we're constantly exploring new flavor combinations and pushing the boundaries of what hot sauce can be.
